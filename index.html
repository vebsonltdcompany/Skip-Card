Skip Card

Classic card game · Offline · No ads · No data collected

Skip Card is a fast-paced and strategic single-player card game. Build piles from 1 → 12, clear your stock first, and win! It’s fully offline, free from ads, and doesn’t collect any data.

How to Play

  1. Play cards in order on the central build piles (1 → 12).
  2. Use Skip cards strategically to change the flow or block opponents.
  3. First to clear their stock pile wins the round!
